Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Our application process involves submitting your academic credentials, a business proposal, and completing an interview with our selection committee. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.

The core program runs for 12 months, with flexible options for extended mentorship and support based on your business needs and progress.

We offer various funding opportunities including seed funding, investor matching, and grant applications. Our team works closely with you to identify and secure the most suitable funding options.
